About Manzil: Your One-Stop Career & Education Guide

We started Manzil with a clear mission: to solve a problem that millions of students and parents face every year. The journey after Class 10 or 12 is often filled with confusion, from choosing the right stream to understanding which course leads to which job. This lack of clarity can lead to poor decisions, dropouts, or an over-reliance on expensive private institutions.

Our goal is to be a one-stop personalized guide for every student. Our platform is designed to provide a clear roadmap that guides confused students toward their goals. We aim to bridge the gap between school, college, and career by explaining long-term outcomes clearly, which helps students see the value in their graduation and prevents dropouts.

Our Unique Approach

  • Equity-Driven: We are specifically designed for students in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities who may have poor internet connectivity. Our app is free and multilingual, ensuring career guidance is accessible to everyone.
  • Localized Career Awareness: Unlike generic platforms, we focus on helping students find pathways through government colleges and nearby opportunities.
  • Holistic Guidance: We combine an aptitude and interest quiz, career mapping (like our visual "career trees"), and a nearby government colleges directory into a single platform.
